# Tier-A Stakes — GARNER

## Anchor

**Feld** anchors **C6 Point-Salad Incommensurability** (adopted). Anchor claim: "5 active Contracts with structurally incompatible scoring dimensions (e.g., C02 penalizes raw Grain while C06 rewards it; C07 rewards raw Barley while C03 rewards converted Malt) make it architecturally impossible to maximize all active contracts simultaneously. The incommensurability is not cosmetic — it affects tile placement from Round 1."

Adjacency partner: **Lacerda on A7** (different persona; designed collision C6↔A7 on temporal-register question). GATE criterion 4 satisfied.

### Vaccarino
1. **A4 Variance Calibration.** 6/7/7. "Two variance sources: (1) contract selection (5-of-20 per game — structurally guaranteed variety); (2) commodity bag draw (per-session tile sequence). 2. **B3 Conversion Chain Depth.** 4/4/4. "2-step pipeline: 2 Grain in same row → 1 Flour; 2 Barley in same column → 1 Malt. Depth-2 is the minimum. The chain is load-bearing for 4+ contracts (C02, C03, C10, C18) but depth-2 is shallower than B3's canonical cases (PR, TtA). Hold."
3. **A5 Downtime/Pacing.** 7/7/7. "Simultaneous placement phase (all players place at once) eliminates the major downtime source. Draft phase is 1 pick per player per round — fast. Conversion is sequential but ~30s per player. GARNER's pacing is strong for a 3.0-weight Euro. Earn."

### Feld (anchor)
1. **C6 Point-Salad Incommensurability (anchor).** **9/9/9.** "5 active contracts create structurally incompatible optimization targets. Example: C02 (+2 VP/Flour, −1 VP/raw Grain) and C07 (+2 VP/Barley, +3 VP to most-Barley) can be active simultaneously — a player processing Barley into Malt for C03 (Flour+Malt pairs) is sacrificing C07 majority. The incommensurability cascades across all 5 contract interactions. No pre-computable dominant strategy. Earn canonical."
2. **A2 Decision Density.** 7/7/8. "Per-turn decision tree: (1) which stall to draft (4 options; others' actions visible); (2) where to place each of up to 4 tiles in 16-space grid (contract-aware placement requires tracking row/column adjacency against active contracts); (3) whether and how many conversions (row/column specificity adds geometric constraint). At 4p, density is highest — others' drafts narrow stall options in real-time. Earn."
3. **B6 Scoring Multiplier Dependency.** 6/6/7. "Three contracts in the deck have majority-bonus multipliers: C07 (+3 VP to most-Barley), C09 (+5 VP to most-Hops), C12 (10 VP to most Processed Stock). When C07 is active alongside contracts rewarding conversion, a player maximizing majority bonuses is in a qualitatively different scoring regime than one ignoring them. The multiplier effect is present and load-bearing when these contracts appear. First B6 earn candidate since TtA (#8). Earn."
