# Tier-A Stakes — WELLSPRING

## Anchor

**Vaccarino** anchors **A4 Variance Calibration** (adopted). Claim: "Pool management is explicit variance calibration: players control which pool to reveal (choosing how much uncertainty to accept per reveal) and where to place purchases (concentrating good cards into one pool increases that pool's reliability). The game's strategic core is adjusting the probability distribution of outcomes, not hoping for luck."

Adjacency partner: **Rosenberg on C4** (different persona). A4↔C4 is the designed collision: variance management philosophy (A4) vs. pool architecture dependency (C4) — OP candidate.
