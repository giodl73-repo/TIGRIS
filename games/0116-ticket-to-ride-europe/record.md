## Collision

**OP: D2↔C1 5-3 (5th TP)**
Spatial-interaction presence (route-blocking on a map of bottlenecks) and tension budget (route-race clock toward card depletion) co-fire through the same claim action. D2 prices the *geography* — that the map has choke points; C1 prices the *clock* — that time-to-claim is rationed. Ticket to Ride Europe earns both because the bottlenecks only matter because the clock is ticking, and the clock only matters because the bottlenecks exist.
Both earn defend marks.
