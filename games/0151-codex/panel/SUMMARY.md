## Argument shape

CODEX is high-gearing, high-catastrophe, high-range. Convocation-as-law (Chvatil B5) is load-bearing. The Veil acts as both tension (C1) and catastrophe (B2), producing a live adjacency. The game refutes the Knizia-triangle (C2) — CODEX deliberately rewards specialization, a design choice, not a failure. The four retire-explicit axes (D3, B3, D1, B4) indicate CODEX's scope: not family-scaling, not deep-conversion, not info-asymmetry, not count-flexible beyond 3-4p. The panel recognized these as axis-inapplicability. The anchor neighborhood (B1 + B5, C4, D4) all earned.
