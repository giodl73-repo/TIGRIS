Composition Notes

The Crown occupies the optical centre — a single hex, gilt-ringed, containing a composite sigil of the four base Schools orbiting a central eye. The four corner sigils rotate through Flame, Tide, Iron, Earth, declaring the core roster without overwhelming the title. The Veil Track sits barely-visible beneath the tagline — a shelf-facing reminder that the game has a clock. Shelf-test heuristic: at three metres the eye should lock onto the central hex and the word CODEX; everything else resolves on approach.
