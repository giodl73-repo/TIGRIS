Map Assembly

Setup: Determine which 4 Schools are playing. Place those Schools' tiles in their fixed corners (Iron NW, Tide NE, Flame SE, Earth SW — or Wind N / Ash S for 6-School games). Shuffle the 6 Variable tiles and place them in the 6 remaining positions. Each Variable tile may be rotated freely.

3-Player variant: Remove 1 Variable tile randomly before placement. The resulting 44-hex map gives more breathing room between Schools.
