The Veil Track

A shared doom clock — 10 steps from full Veil to Unraveling. The Marker advances after every round and accelerates on engine growth events. Its position is public information at all times.

VEIL TRACK
1 per game · 280 × 60 mm strip · Spine of the arc
The Veil Track defines the game's pressure envelope. It starts at 10 and counts to 0; when it reaches 0 the Unraveling begins. Depth accelerates doom — building a 5th component, activating 3+ Forges, or destroying via Conflict each add +1 advance. The track carries Veil Event card slots so flipped effects are legible at all player counts.
Advance Triggers
  • +1 after every round (automatic)
  • +1 when any player builds their 5th component
  • +1 when any player activates 3+ Forges in a round
  • +1 when a Conflict action destroys a component
Veil Events (steps 7, 5, 3, 1)
  • Flip top card of 20-card Veil Event deck
  • Effect active until start of next round
  • Cards are not reshuffled — order matters
  • Veil's Eye hex slows advance by 1 while controlled
Physical Specification — Manufacturing Guidance

Material and dimensional recommendations per component type. Defaults prioritise tactile differentiation — players must distinguish component types by touch alone in a crowded board state.

Component Material Footprint Thickness Notes
Forge Wood 22 × 25 mm hex 10 mm Heaviest build piece; beech or birch with screen-printed gilt border and icon. Weight sells the "industrial" silhouette on table.
Sanctum Wood 20 × 24 mm dome 9 mm Custom-milled domed octagon; two-pass paint (base School colour, gilt outline). Stackable for VP accumulation.
Channel Acrylic 28 × 14 mm oval 4 mm Translucent acrylic with etched flow-lines; layered on hex tile to read as "overlay" not "blocker". 3mm alternative for cost target.
Spire Wood 14 × 28 mm tri 8 mm Tall narrow triangle; lathe-turned if budget allows, otherwise die-cut. Height differential from Forges must be visible across table.
Iron Token MetalCardboard Ø 24 mm 2 mm (metal) / 2.5 mm (card) Premium edition: zinc-alloy with antique-gold finish. Standard: 2.5mm punchboard with gilt-foil stamp. Must clink.
VP Token Cardboard Ø 16 mm 2 mm Small parchment discs, laminated matte. Denominations 1/5/10 via printed pip clusters; never by size (players miscount).
Veil Marker Acrylic 20 × 20 mm diamond 5 mm Gilt-laminated acrylic, single per game. Tall profile ensures visibility — the Veil's position is the game's clock.
Veil Track Cardboard 280 × 60 mm strip 2 mm 10-step track strip (steps 10→0 left to right). Steps 7, 5, 3, 1 carry Veil Event card slots (small recessed frame, 45 × 30 mm). Steps 4–0 printed on a warm-red tint to signal critical zone. Single gilt-foil border. Matte laminate on face; gloss on card-slot recesses to distinguish. Print in two layers: base track offset-printed, Veil Event slot frames spot-UV. Recommended: fold-out board extension or separate strip seated in the main board margin.
