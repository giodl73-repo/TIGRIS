# Race for the Galaxy — Design (Rules Import)

## Core loop

Each round: (1) All players simultaneously select 1 role tile. (2) All selected roles execute, in a fixed order. Non-selected roles are skipped. (3) Each player who selected a role gets a bonus when it executes (e.g., selecting Explore draws extra cards).

## Roles

- **Explore:** Draw 2+ cards, keep 1 (bonus: draw more).
- **Develop:** Play a Development card by discarding cards = its cost. (bonus: −1 cost)
- **Settle:** Play a Planet card. (bonus: draw 1 card after)
- **Consume:** Consume goods on planets for VP tokens. (bonus: 2×VP)
- **Produce:** Produce goods on planets. (bonus: produce on a windfall planet)
- **Trade:** Sell one good for cards. (bonus: draw 1 extra card)
- **Colonize:** Military settle a military planet. (bonus: force military value +1)

## Cards

Each card is a planet (with terrain = military strength OR settlement cost) or development (immediate power/VP). Cards also function as currency (discard to pay costs). 109 unique cards.

## Tableau engine

Cards in tableau provide: powers (when specific roles execute), VP values, goods production, consumption. Engine-building: a well-constructed tableau executes actions more efficiently than a thin one.

## Victory

Game ends when any player plays 12+ cards, or VP token supply exhausts. Score: card VP + VP tokens + bonuses from 6-development cards.

## Weight profile

Weight 2.99. Teach time 25 min (icon-heavy; player reference card essential). High replayability from card permutations + role-selection interaction.
