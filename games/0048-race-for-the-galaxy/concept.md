# Race for the Galaxy — Concept

## Premise

Race for the Galaxy is a 2–4 player simultaneous-action-selection card game. Players draft and play cards from a shared tableau of planets and developments. Each round, all players secretly select one of 7 roles (Explore, Develop, Settle, Consume, Produce, Trade, Colonize); all selected roles execute for all players. Unselected roles do nothing. The card game's innovation: the game's "board" is your tableau of played cards, and the "actions" are determined by what roles players collectively select.

Vaccarino anchors on A4 Variance Calibration — the role-selection creates calibrated variance: knowing opponents' likely role selections lets you plan around the phase permutation, but you can never be certain what will execute.
