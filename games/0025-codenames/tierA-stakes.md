# Tier-A Stakes — Codenames

## Anchor

**Chvátil** anchors **B5 Architectural Novelty** (adopted; monitoring at 2.0R). Claim: "Codenames' information-architecture — one-word clue encoding semantic overlap across a shared grid while avoiding Assassin and enemy words — is novel in the party/word genre. No prior game requires a Spymaster to solve: 'find one word that semantically spans N target words without touching M enemy words or 1 instant-loss word.' The mechanism is architecturally unprecedented at party-game weight."

Adjacency partner: **Rosenberg on A7** (different persona). B5↔A7 is the designed collision: is the one-word-clue architecture (B5) orthogonal to across-play replayability (A7)?
