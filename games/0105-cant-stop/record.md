## Collision

**OP: B2↔A4 6-2 (6th TP, new pair)**
Bust-catastrophe (triple-column failure zeroes gains) and variance calibration (column-length tuned to two-dice probability distribution) both live in the same dice-pair mechanic but score different registers. B2 prices the *catastrophe* on failure; A4 prices how the *distribution* is calibrated so the catastrophe is interesting. Can't Stop earns both — the calibration *makes* the catastrophe engaging rather than random.
Both earn defend marks.
