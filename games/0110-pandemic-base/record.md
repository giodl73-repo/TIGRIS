## Collision

**OP: B2↔A3 5-3 (5th TP)**
Outbreak catastrophe (escalating chain) and interaction (mandatory cooperative interaction) sit in the same moment — a city outbreak forces everyone to re-plan. B2 prices the *mechanical* catastrophe curve; A3 prices that the curve *forces* interaction rather than allowing parallel play. Pandemic earns both because outbreaks wouldn't produce cooperation without mandatory interaction, and the interaction wouldn't be load-bearing without catastrophes demanding it.
Both earn defend marks.
