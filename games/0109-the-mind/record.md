## Collision

**OP: A26↔A3 6-2 (6th TP)**
Social contract (no-speaking rule) and interaction (mandatory cooperative attention) are both maximal and usually fused in description, but score different registers. A26 prices *what kind of norm* governs communication (here: none); A3 prices *the density of required interpersonal attention* (here: total). The Mind earns both extreme — silence is a contract *and* the cooperation is nonstop.
Both earn defend marks.
