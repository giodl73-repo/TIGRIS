# Perihelion — Concept

**Anchor:** Feld on C6 Incommensurable Scoring. Three orbital zones (Inner Belt, Gas Giants, Deep Space) score through entirely distinct phenomena chains. You cannot optimize all three — your trajectory commitment forces you to specialize, and the scoring formulas cannot be reduced to a single value-comparison. First rondel game in TIGRIS corpus.

**Mechanic:** 2–4 players, 45 minutes. Rondel where each step represents an orbital maneuver: gravity-assist (one step, cheap, constrained) or direct burn (skip steps, expensive, flexible). Players occupy orbital slots and collect phenomena tokens from adjacent zones. End-game: score per zone formula.
