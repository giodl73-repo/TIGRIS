## Collision

**OP: A1↔C6 6-2 (6th TP)**
Elegance (compact draft-place-score cycle) and point-salad incommensurability (six distinct star paths with incomparable endgame bonuses) usually trade off, but Summer Pavilion earns both — the scoring complexity is *expressed* through the elegant rule. A1 prices the rule-surface; C6 prices the scoring-path incommensurability. Both earn because the elegance doesn't collapse the variety, and the variety doesn't inflate the rule surface.
Both earn defend marks.
