B2 fires. +1 defend."

**A3 (Chvátil) — Defend**: "River Spirit (P1) can slow the Coastal Forest (reduce Invaders) but cannot reach Mountain. A3 = mandatory cooperative structure. +1 defend."

### B2↔A3 COLLISION — Round 5 (OP, temporal-register)

**Feld (B2 anchor) proposes OP**: "B2 and A3 fire on the Invader cascade mechanism at different analytical registers. B2 asks: how severe and frequent are the reversal events? It evaluates the catastrophe architecture — the Blight cascade, the escalating Invader build progression, the Fear track as ticking threat. B2 = what happens when you fail to defend. A3 asks: how mandatory is the player-to-player interaction? It evaluates the cooperative structure — Spirits must cover different territories, coordinate power timing, chain responses. A3 = what happens when you succeed through cooperation. B2 asks about failure; A3 asks about coordination. Different questions."
