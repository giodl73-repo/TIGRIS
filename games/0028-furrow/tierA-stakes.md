# Tier-A Stakes — FURROW

## Anchor

**Chvátil** anchors **B5 Architectural Novelty** (adopted; monitoring). Claim: "Trick-as-action-selection: winning a trick chooses which crop you harvest. Standard trick-taking determines who scores; FURROW determines what you score and of which type. This structural distinction — the lead suit defines the economic stakes of the trick, not just its scoring — is novel in the trick-taking genre."

Adjacency partners: **Lacerda on A7** (temporal-register OP candidate; B5↔A7) and **Chvátil self-adjacency via A3** (second collision: B5↔A3 OP candidate). Two collision candidates; gate passes on A7 (different persona).
