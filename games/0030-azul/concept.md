# Azul — Concept (review-import)

## Anchor declaration

**Anchor persona:** Kramer-Kiesling (K-K)
**Anchor axis:** A1 Elegance (adopted)
**Anchor claim:** "Azul achieves the highest rule-count/depth ratio in the TIGRIS corpus. Rules: draft tiles from factory displays, fill pattern lines, complete rows to score. Total rules: ≈ 8. Depth: drafting strategy, color management, row completion timing, penalty avoidance, blocking opponents — profound strategic space from minimal machinery. Kiesling's own game. K-K own-anchor on a BGG top-10 game."

**Secondary focus:** Pure-abstract gap closure. TIGRIS corpus had 0 pure-abstract reviews pre-#30 (Codenames is party/word; Tikal is thematic; CoB is tile-placement-but-thematic). Azul is the canonical pure-abstract Euro.

## Context

- K-K own-anchor (Kiesling without Kramer; counted as K-K own-anchor for simplicity).
- 2–4 players; BGG weight 1.77; ~30–45 min.
- Closes the pure-abstract gap.
- Tests B5 monitoring status: Azul's mechanism is borrowed from Portuguese azulejo tile patterns — it's not architecturally novel, but K-K might stake B5 to test.

## Expected signals

**Earn-candidates:**
- **A1 Elegance (anchor; K-K)** — canonical. Rule-count/depth ratio is Azul's defining achievement.
- **A3 Interaction (Chvátil)** — strong: drafting from shared factory displays; taking a color depletes it for opponents; blocking is explicit and deliberate.
- **D3 Count-Robustness (Stegmaier)** — 2–4 players; game plays identically at all counts.
- **C7 Action-Menu Clarity (K-K)** — draft action is maximally clear: pick color from one display, fill a pattern line, overflow to floor.
- **A4 Variance Calibration (Vaccarino)** — tile bag draw + factory seeding = managed randomness.
- **C1 Tension Budget (Knizia)** — end-round wall-scoring + penalty floor creates tension clock.
- **A6 Teachability (Stegmaier)** — Azul is a teaching standard; under 10 min to learn.

**B5 monitoring test:** K-K may draft B5. Azul's mechanism (tile-draft → pattern-line → wall-score) is borrowed from azulejo tile art tradition; no novelty claim. Retire-explicit expected. B5 monitoring clock: earned at FURROW #28, 2 games ago — not a concern.

**Retire-explicit candidates (heavy):** B2, B3, C3, C4, C6, D2, D4 — pure-abstract games have minimal Euro-specific mechanism overlap.
