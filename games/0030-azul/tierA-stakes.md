# Tier-A Stakes — Azul

## Anchor

**Kramer-Kiesling** anchors **A1 Elegance** (adopted). Claim: "Azul achieves the highest rule-count/depth ratio in the TIGRIS corpus at its weight. Total rules: draft from factory OR center; follow suit or overflow to floor; tile wall on completion; score adjacency. Under 10 rules. Depth: factory sequencing, color blocking, pattern-line commitment, wall placement geometry. BGG weight 1.77 with 20+ hours of strategic variety."

Adjacency partner: **Chvátil on A3** (different persona). A1↔A3 OP candidate: structural mechanism quality (A1) vs. social dynamic of blocking (A3).
