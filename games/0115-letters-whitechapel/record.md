## Collision

**OP: B4↔A28 6-2 (6th TP)**
Information-transparency cost (hidden-Jack movement costs detectives their action budget to uncover) and cognitive-load profile (pure deductive-reasoning type) both anchor the game. B4 prices the *mechanical* price of information; A28 prices the *type* of thinking the game demands. Both earn — the info-pricing mechanism forces the deductive register, but a redesigned game could use the same info-pricing with a different cognitive type.
Both earn defend marks.
