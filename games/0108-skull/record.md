## Collision

**OP: A26↔B2 5-3 (5th TP)**
Bluff social contract (disk semantics governed by convention) and catastrophe pressure (flipping own skull ends round disastrously) co-fire at the same moment but score different registers. A26 prices the *norm-layer*; B2 prices the *failure-trigger*. Skull earns both — the bluff only feels stakes-laden because the catastrophe is real, and the catastrophe only reads as suspense because the bluff norm lets it stay hidden.
Both earn defend marks.
