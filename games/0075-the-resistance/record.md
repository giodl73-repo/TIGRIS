## Collision

**OP: A26↔B4 6-2 (6th TP)**
A26 scores the norm-dependent lying culture the game demands; B4 scores the explicit hidden-role information cost baked into the mechanics. Resistance proves these are separable: a group can have strong social norms yet weak mechanical hidden-info cost (Werewolf) or vice versa. The Resistance earns both.
Both earn defend marks.
