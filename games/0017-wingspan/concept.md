# Wingspan — Concept

## Anchor declaration

**Anchor persona:** Vaccarino
**Anchor axis:** C4 Engine-Garden Dependency (adopted)
**Anchor claim:** "Bird tableau IS the engine-garden. Each bird in each habitat row becomes an activated card that feeds others. Vaccarino's deck-building genre refactored as card-tableau-engine without deck cycling — my signature mechanism applied to a non-Dominion frame. Third consecutive non-own anchor."

**Secondary focus 1:** Chvátil self-refutes **A3 Interaction** — Wingspan's multiplayer-solitaire critique is structural and well-documented. Target: **2nd adopted-axis refute across 16 games** (after A7 in AFoS). First multi-axis test of v2.14 adopted-contested pathway.

**Secondary focus 2:** Lacerda defends **A7 Emergence-Replayability** with Wingspan's 170-bird-card corpus — counterweight against AFoS Halifax Hammer refute. Protects A7 adoption status.
