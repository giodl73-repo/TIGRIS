## Collision

**OP: C2↔A1 5-3 (5th TP)**
Minimum-score shape (unfilled-square penalty enforces protection of a floor) and elegance (two-sentence ruleset) often correlate in light fillers but score different things. C2 prices whether a *scoring-floor* mechanic exists and binds; A1 prices whether the *rule surface* is elegant. Patchwork Doodle earns both — its elegance is partly a function of the floor rule's compactness.
Both earn defend marks.
