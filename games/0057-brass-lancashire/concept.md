# Brass: Lancashire — Concept (Review Mode)

## Published game summary

Brass: Lancashire is a 2–4 player economic network-building game (120–150 min, weight 4.0). Players develop industries in Industrial Revolution England, building coal mines, cotton mills, and ports/shipyards along canal and rail networks. The game is played in two eras: Canal era (first half) and Rail era (second half). At era transition, all canal-era buildings are removed from the board — only buildings consumed (fully linked into the network) survive into the Rail era. The epoch transition is the game's definitive late-game lock-in mechanism.

**BGG rank:** consistently in top 5 (BGG ID 28720). Designer: Martin Wallace. Publisher: Warfrog (2007); Roxley (2018 deluxe edition).
