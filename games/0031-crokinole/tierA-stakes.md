# Tier-A Stakes — Crokinole

## Anchor

**K-K** anchors **D2 Spatial-Interaction Presence** (adopted). Claim: "Crokinole is the purest D2 game in the TIGRIS corpus. The spatial interaction is not mediated by mechanisms — you physically flick discs at opponents' discs on a shared board. The spatial geometry of disc positions directly determines scoring and the next player's options. D2's canonical earning condition is exactly this."

Adjacency partner: **Chvátil on A3**. D2↔A3 OP candidate (9th temporal-register OP attempt; same pattern as WEALD).
