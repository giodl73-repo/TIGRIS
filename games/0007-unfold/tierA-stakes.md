## Stakes (compact)

### Chvátil (anchor)

| Axis | 3p | 4p | Justification |
|---|---:|---:|---|
| **B5 Architectural Novelty** (anchor; adopted) | 10 | 10 | UNFOLD's 4-system gearing (pop-up state × forced-fold cascade × culture lens × Dev Level) is the novel architectural bet. Forced-fold cascade specifically exploits 3D physicality in a way no prior game does. Canonical case for the axis that UNFOLD was anchored on. |
| A3 Interaction | 8 | 9 | Forced-fold cascade means every action affects an adjacent tile (often opponent's strategy). Shared Dev Level track creates collision between cultures' ideals. Spirit displacement adds direct contest. 4p has more cross-player cascade targets. |
| B6 Scoring Multiplier Dependency (fallback) | 6 | 6 | Dev-Alignment multiplier (×0.5 to ×1.5 based on distance from ideal) is a genuine endgame multiplier. Less intricate than Scythe's 6-factor or Puerto Rico's stacking multipliers; simpler single-axis. |

### Vaccarino

| Axis | 3p | 4p | Justification |
|---|---:|---:|---|
| A4 Variance Calibration | 3 | 3 | UNFOLD has LOW variance: tile draw from bag is the only randomness; no dice, no event cards. Starting seed scenarios (3-4 variants) add some. Diagnostic-low — UNFOLD is a design choice in favor of deterministic strategy. |
| C4 Engine-Garden (adopted) | 5 | 5 | Limited engine-building. Cultures' tools are small modifiers, not combos. Buildings don't chain into other buildings. Below Dominion/Puerto Rico earning. |
| A5 Downtime-Pacing (fallback) | 6 | 5 | 2 actions per turn is fast. But 4p creates 3-player waits between your turns; Dusk phases synchronize but also pause. Mild downtime at 4p. |

### Knizia

| Axis | 3p | 4p | Justification |
|---|---:|---:|---|
| **C1 Tension Budget** | 8 | 8 | 2 actions per turn. Forced-fold cascade requires every decision to be a two-tile decision. Dev Level thresholds and Dusk obligations create periodic tension. Every turn ends with the player knowing what they can't do. Matches canonical tension-budget pattern. |
| C2 Min-Score Shape (adopted) | 4 | 4 | UNFOLD uses raw cultural score × Dev-Alignment multiplier, not min-score. Adopted axis doesn't apply here; diagnostic-low. |
| A1 Elegance | 7 | 7 | 4 action types + forced-fold + 4 cultures + Dev Level track. Compact rules (15 sections in rulebook) but 4 interacting systems challenges one-page summary. Above Parliament elegance but below Puerto Rico. |

### Feld

| Axis | 3p | 4p | Justification |
|---|---:|---:|---|
| **C6 Point-Salad Incommensurability** | 8 | 8 | 4 cultures × 4 distinct scoring formulas × Dev-Alignment modifier = genuinely incommensurable. Same island scores 4 radically different ways. Stronger point-salad than Scythe; on par with Puerto Rico buildings. |
| A2 Decision Density (adopted) | 8 | 9 | Every action is a 2-tile decision (cascade). Plus culture lens, plus Dev-Level impact, plus potential Dusk threshold. Dense per-turn decisions. Higher at 4p due to more cross-player effects. |
| B2 Catastrophe Pressure | 4 | 4 | Dusk Phase at Dev thresholds is a periodic obligation (Rosenberg feeding-equivalent), not catastrophe. No reversal events that destroy progress. Below threshold. |

### Lacerda

| Axis | 3p | 4p | Justification |
|---|---:|---:|---|
| **B1 System Gearing** (adopted) | 9 | 9 | 4 subsystems (state × cascade × culture × Dev-Level) gear multiplicatively. Remove any and UNFOLD becomes a different game. Validation pass confirmed 4-for-4 Lisboa test. Canonical case. |
| B4 Info-Transparency-Cost (adopted) | 4 | 4 | UNFOLD has mostly-public state: tile placements visible, Dev Level public, Spirits visible. Opponents' next moves hidden but no strong information asymmetry. Diagnostic-low for this game. |
| A7 Emergence-Replayability (adopted) | 8 | 9 | 3-4 starting seeds × 4 culture picks × placement variance = high replayability. Per-seat asymmetric setup at game start. 25+ session variations easily. |

### Rosenberg

| Axis | 3p | 4p | Justification |
|---|---:|---:|---|
| C3 Scarcity Bite | 6 | 7 | Tile bag finite. Dusk phase adds periodic scarcity-management. Not feeding-phase-hard; closer to Puerto Rico's colonist-scarcity. |
| D4 Late-Game Lock-in (adopted) | 7 | 7 | As Dev Level approaches 40, Cultural strategies lock. Canonical ⅔ lock-in point. |
| B3 Conversion Chain Depth | 4 | 4 | UNFOLD's conversion is tile-state → Dev contribution → Dev-Alignment score. Shallow chain (2 steps). Below Puerto Rico. |

### K-K

| Axis | 3p | 4p | Justification |
|---|---:|---:|---|
| C7 Action-Menu Clarity | 7 | 7 | 4 action types (PLACE/FLIP/MOVE SPIRIT/USE TOOL). Clear on card. But forced-fold cascade adds mid-action complexity not in the menu. Near-earning. |

| **D2 Spatial-Interaction Presence** (adopted) | 9 | 9 | Shared island board + adjacency rules + cascade cross-player effects + Spirit placement = heavy spatial interaction. On par with T&E, stronger than Scythe. |
