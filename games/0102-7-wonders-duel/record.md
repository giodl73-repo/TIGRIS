## Collision

**OP: C6↔A2 6-2 (6th TP)**
Point-salad incommensurability (four parallel track types including two instant-wins) and decision density (per-card branching) are both high — but score different things. C6 prices the existence of non-comparable victory axes; A2 prices the per-turn branching factor. 7WD earns both: the four tracks *create* per-turn branching rather than being collapsed by it.
Both earn defend marks.
