# The Crew: Quest for Planet Nine — Concept

**Anchor:** Stegmaier on A6 Teachability. The mission-card structure is a Stonemaier-paradigm teachability innovation: 50 missions of escalating complexity use the same base rules, teaching through play rather than front-loading. Cooperative trick-taking is documented as a contrast to FURROW (#28) competitive trick-taking.

**Expected:** A6 (anchor), A3 (mandatory communication: cooperative trick-taking forces team coordination), A1 (elegant cooperative trick-taking), D3, C7 (lead/follow/trump = clear menu), C1 (mission completion tension per trick), A4 (hand distribution variance), A5 (fast tricks), D1 (family-accessible). Collision: A6↔A3 OP (cooperative teachability vs. mandatory communication interaction).
