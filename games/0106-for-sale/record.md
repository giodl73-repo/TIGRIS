## Collision

**OP: C1↔A2 6-2 (6th TP)**
Tension budget (bid escalation) and decision density (per-bid choice calculus) co-locate in the same action but score different things. C1 prices the *accumulating* pressure across an auction; A2 prices per-bid live choice complexity. For Sale earns both — a bid decision is simultaneously one tick on the tension budget and one loaded decision-point.
Both earn defend marks.
