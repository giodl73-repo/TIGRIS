# Terra Mystica — Concept

## Premise

Terra Mystica is a 2–5 player asymmetric area-expansion game where 14 different factions compete on a hex-grid terrain board. Each faction can only build on its own terrain type; transforming adjacent terrain costs terraforming workers. Six rounds of building, upgrading, and gathering resources create a multiplayer engine-building web where all scoring paths are incommensurable.

Feld anchors on C6 Point-Salad Incommensurability — Terra Mystica is the apex point-salad: territory expansion, power cycles, favor tiles, bonus cards, round-end scoring, final scoring (buildings/merchant/fortress + area + cult tracks × 3) are genuine non-substitutable VP sources.

## Players and length

- **Players:** 2–5
- **Length:** 60–150 min
- **Weight:** 3.98

## Anchor

**Feld on C6 Point-Salad Incommensurability** — the scoring paths (territory, cult, power, buildings, area, round-scoring) cannot be directly compared. A cult-focused Auren vs. a terraforming-focused Halflings vs. a power-cycling Witches are playing different games that share a board.

**Collision candidate: C6↔B1** — are Terra Mystica's scoring dimensions (C6) actually just System Gearing (B1)? Or do the VP dimensions fire on genuinely distinct registers?
