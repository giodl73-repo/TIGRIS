## Session 1

**Teach time**: 4 minutes 30 seconds. Rules summary to 4 players: "Move around the hex map. Gather resources from terrain. Build structures. Keep the Threat counter below 8. Build 2 structures before the threat gets out of hand — you win."

**A6 (Stegmaier anchor) — Defend**: "Session 1 completes its teach in under 5 minutes, with 4 adults who have never seen the game. No role complexity (only 2 roles, both simple). The teach is self-contained. HEARTH is among the fastest-teaching cooperative games in the corpus. +1 defend on A6."
