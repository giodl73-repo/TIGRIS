# Quacks of Quedlinburg — Rules Summary

## 1. Overview
2–4 players draw ingredient tokens from personal bags to fill cauldrons. Each round: draw tokens one at a time; stop when satisfied (score high) or keep drawing (risk bust). Cherry bomb tokens are the bust trigger: 7+ cherry bombs = explode; you score but get no victory points from this round's draw. End-game: 9 rounds; highest cumulative VP wins.

## 2. Key Mechanics
- **Bag-building**: buy new ingredient tokens each round; add to bag; next round's probabilities shift
- **Push-your-luck**: keep drawing = higher position on the scoring track; bust = lose VP but keep position
- **Cherry bomb rule**: each cherry bomb = 1 space; ≥7 = bust. You control your own bust risk by knowing how many cherry bombs are in your bag
- **Ingredient effects**: different ingredient types trigger bonuses (e.g., pumpkins double a draw if placed adjacent; spiders give bonus movement)
- **Formula books**: each player uses one of 4 formula books (asymmetric ingredient values) — creates different optimal strategies
