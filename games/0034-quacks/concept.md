# Quacks of Quedlinburg — Concept

**Anchor:** Feld on B2 Catastrophe Pressure. The "bust" mechanic (too many cherry bombs = explode = forfeit round) is the game's defining catastrophe — an escalating push-your-luck pressure building within each round. Closes bag-building mechanism gap.

**Expected:** B2 (anchor), A4 (bag variance), C1 (bust pressure clock), A6 (teachable push-your-luck), D1 (scaling), D3 (count-robust), A2 (per-draw decisions). Collision: B2↔A4 OP (catastrophe vs. variance management).
