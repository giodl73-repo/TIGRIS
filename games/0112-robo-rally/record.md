## Collision

**OP: A27↔A28 5-3 (5th TP)**
Physical affordance (slotting cards into five registers) and cognitive-load profile (spatial-sequential reasoning type) both peak in RoboRally but score different registers. A27 is about the material / procedural substrate — the act of laying cards down; A28 is about the *kind of thinking* the game requires — spatial sequence projection. Both earn separately: a mental-only version would still be heavy cognitive load; a physical-only version would lack the spatial projection demand.
Both earn defend marks.
