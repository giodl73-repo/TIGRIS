# SOLSTICE — Concept

## Milestone Game #50 — Knizia OWN-ANCHOR

SOLSTICE is a 2–4 player Euro game where the ENTIRE game IS a tension budget made elegant. The simplest scoring structure in the TIGRIS corpus. The canonical reference design for both A1 Elegance and C1 Tension Budget simultaneously.

## Premise

SOLSTICE is a seasonal card-game. Each game spans 4 Seasons (rounds). Each Season, players simultaneously place 3 cards face-down across 5 Arenas. Cards reveal; each Arena awards its card-type bonus. A player's final score is their MINIMUM across all 5 Arenas plus 1 VP per card played to the Arena they scored highest in (the "Solstice Bonus" fires once, at the Arena where you're strongest).

The design bets are:
1. **A1 Elegance** — the minimum-score + Solstice Bonus creates the entire strategic space. Rules: 5 Arenas, 4 Seasons, 15 cards, 1 rule (minimum determines floor; strongest Arena gets Solstice). That's the entire game.
2. **C1 Tension Budget** — 15 cards, 4 Seasons, 5 Arenas. You can fully cover 3 Arenas, partially cover 5, or over-commit to 2. Every card allocation forecloses another option.
3. The MINIMUM rule means you must diversify (neglected Arenas drag your score), but the SOLSTICE BONUS rewards specialization (your strongest Arena gets +VP). This tension between diversify-to-raise-minimum vs. specialize-for-bonus is the entire game in one rule.

## Players and length

- **Players:** 2–4
- **Length:** 30–45 min
- **Weight:** 2.0

## Anchor

**Knizia own-anchor on A1 Elegance** — SOLSTICE is designed to be the canonical reference for what "rule-count-to-depth ratio" means at its extreme. One minimum rule + one Solstice bonus rule + 15 cards = the complete strategic space. It is more elegant than Patchwork (which earns A1 at weight 1.85) because the rules are fewer and the depth is comparable.

**Dual-axis bet:** SOLSTICE is designed to earn BOTH A1 and C1 canonically in the same game — the first TIGRIS design to target both axes as co-anchors with equal weight. Knizia defends A1 as anchor and earns C1 via the designed tension.

**Additional target:** A2, A6, B2, C3, C6, A3, A7, D3, D4 (12-axis earn target from prompt spec).

## Non-goals

- Not a pure minimalist game (a few special card powers keep it interesting).
- Does not claim B5 novelty (minimum-score designs exist: Ingenious, T&E).
- Does not claim B1 gearing (single-subsystem by design).
