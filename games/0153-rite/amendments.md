## A32 ADOPTION — v2.24.85

**A32 Information Architecture Type** is formally adopted.

- Game 1: ESTUARY #152 (Chvátil; hidden-valuations/obligation type; score 7)
- Game 2: RITE #153 (Lacerda; hidden-state with involuntary physical leakage; score 7-8)

**Two distinct subtypes confirmed in corpus:**
1. Hidden-valuations/obligation (ESTUARY): known objects with unknown mandatory
   obligations. Edict mechanic reveals obligation structure.
2. Hidden-state with involuntary leakage (RITE): hidden progression state that leaks
   through mandatory physical reactions. Cannot be masked; only managed.

Both score A32=7-8. Both are above private hands (A32=3) and below inverted visibility
(A32=10). The axis earns on structural information type, not surface theme.

**A32 adoption note:** The two A32 earns are from games in the same design session
(2026-04-23). This does not disqualify adoption — the threshold is "2 different games,"
met by ESTUARY (#152) and RITE (#153). However, the Parliament notes this adjacency
as a flag for future review: if both earns came from the same design session's
conceptual cluster, the corpus may be over-sampling one information subtype. The next
A32 earn should come from a design with no lineage to either ESTUARY or RITE.
