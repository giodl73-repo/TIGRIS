## Argument Shape

RITE's Parliament record establishes the E-band as a viable axis band for TIGRIS
originals — 6 of 13 earned stakes are in Band E. This is new. Prior originals (ESTUARY,
CODEX, SOLSTICE) earned primarily on A/B/C bands. RITE demonstrates that a designed
social-physical game earns on the experiential axes the euro tradition suppresses.

Gap 1 (E+R) is now represented in the TIGRIS corpus for the first time. RITE scores
A26=9, A27=10, A25=9 (E dimension) and A7=9 (R dimension). The PCA would place RITE
at approximately E=8.5, R=8 — directly in the Gap 1 target zone.

The Officiant mechanic is the design's architectural contribution: rotating hierarchy
that simultaneously grants power (Convene, Challenge tokens) and exposes the holder
(everyone watches the Officiant). The social contract flows through the hierarchy.
