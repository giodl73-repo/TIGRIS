# Twilight Struggle — Concept

## Anchor declaration

**Anchor persona:** Knizia
**Anchor axis:** C1 Tension Budget
**Anchor claim:** "DEFCON track + forced scoring cards = textbook tension economy. Every ops point is leveraged against a brinksmanship ceiling. Pure Knizia-territory tension even though Knizia didn't design it."

**Secondary focus:** Stegmaier pushing **C8 First-Turn Compression** as retirement-decision test.

## Context

- First non-Euro in TIGRIS. Card-driven wargame (CDG). 2-player only.
- No designer-on-own anchor possible — Gupta/Matthews are not in the 8-persona roster.
- Review stress-tests player-count axes (D1/D3) and single-VP-track axes (C6).
- C8 has 0 earnings across 14 games — TS opening is canonical stress case.

## Expected signals

- C1 Tension Budget (anchor; Knizia) — canonical; DEFCON = tension made literal.
- B5 Architectural Novelty (Chvátil) — CDG genre-founding in mass-market; Hannibal (1996) preceded but TS mainstreamed.
- C8 First-Turn Compression (Stegmaier) — USSR's Turn 1 coup windows are heavily studied/pre-optimized.
- B2 Catastrophe Pressure (Feld) — DEFCON-1 = instant loss; nuclear war is the catastrophe.
- A3 Interaction (Chvátil) — 100% zero-sum; literally only interaction.
- A4 Variance Calibration (Vaccarino) — card deal + headline flip significant variance.
- A7 Emergence (Lacerda) — 100+ cards × order × Space Race = vast.
- B4 Information-Transparency-Cost (Lacerda) — hand-reveal events; scoring-card timing.
- B1 System Gearing (Lacerda) — ops → influence → presence → dominance → control.
