# DELUGE — Concept

## Premise

DELUGE is a 2–4 player flood-management Euro where water permanently encroaches on farmland. Each round, flood markers advance along river tributaries; players must harvest their fields before they are permanently inundated. Flooded tiles are removed from the game — arable land shrinks with each round.

C3 anchors because the scarcity bite is designed and permanent: floods don't merely reduce available actions this round — they permanently remove farmland from the game. By round 6, large portions of the board may be unplayable. Players who over-invest in a region that floods lose everything.

## Anchor mechanic

**C3 Scarcity Bite.** Permanent flood markers on farmland tiles. Once flooded, a tile cannot be harvested. The flood advance is partially predictable (flood markers spread along river tributaries) but the exact spread depends on rainfall cards drawn each round. Players must harvest before floods arrive — the scarcity is not just resource-limited but time-limited. Fields that flood before harvest = 0 VP for all investment made there.

**D4 secondary** — permanent flood placement locks strategy. Once flood markers are placed, they cannot be removed. Players committing to distant farm regions that will flood by round 5 have locked in a losing strategy by round 3.
