## Collision

**OP: D2↔A1 6-2 (6th TP)**
Spatial-interaction presence (bamboo-garden tile adjacency + panda/gardener co-positioning) and elegance (tight three-action menu plus weather die) are both strong. D2 prices the *spatial* system; A1 prices the *rule-surface*. Takenoko earns both because the spatial richness is produced by the elegant menu rather than by exception-cases.
Both earn defend marks.
