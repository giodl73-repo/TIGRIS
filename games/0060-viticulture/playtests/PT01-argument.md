### B1↔D4 COLLISION — Year 3 (OP, temporal-register)

**Lacerda (B1 stake) proposes OP**: "B1 fires on the production pipeline — the causal dependency chain Vine→Harvest→Crush→Wine→Order where each stage depends on the prior. B1 is the architecture of how wine is made at any given moment. D4 fires on temporal commitment — the cellar aging decision commits a resource to a multi-year wait. B1 = production architecture; D4 = temporal investment timing. Different registers: B1 is about structural dependency; D4 is about the when of strategic commitment."

**Rosenberg (D4 stake) seconds OP**: "D4 asks: at what fraction of game length does strategy commit? B1 asks: how many stages of dependency does the production pipeline have? These are different questions. A game can have a long B1 pipeline with no late D4 lock-in (immediate wine-sell). A game can have strong D4 commitment without a long pipeline. They decouple."

**Result: 6-2 OP** (Lacerda + Rosenberg + Knizia + Chvátil + K-K + Stegmaier = 6 OP; Feld + Vaccarino = 2 CR). **33rd temporal-register OP.** B1 and D4 both earn.
