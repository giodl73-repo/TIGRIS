# Hanabi — Concept (Review Mode)

## Published game summary

Hanabi is a 2–5 player cooperative card game (25 min, weight 1.7) with inverted hand visibility: you hold your cards facing away from you, so you can see everyone else's hand but not your own. Players collaboratively build 5 fireworks sequences (colors 1→5). Each turn, a player gives a color or number clue (spending a clue token), plays a card from hand, or discards a card (returning a clue token). Plays must be in sequence (1 before 2, etc.); misplays lose a fuse token. Three fuse tokens lost = game over.

**BGG rank:** Top 100; Spiel des Jahres winner 2013 (BGG ID 98778). Designer: Antoine Bauza.
