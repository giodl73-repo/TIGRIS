# Lisboa — Concept (imported for review)

## Premise

Review of *Lisboa* (Vital Lacerda, 2017). Rebuilding Lisbon after the 1755 earthquake, tsunami, and fire. Lacerda's canonical subsystem-gearing game; card-driven multi-action economy; no randomness beyond card draw.

- **Players:** 1-4 (3p optimal; 4p stretches; 2p is a different game)
- **Length:** 120-180 min (2-2.5 hr at 3p with experienced players)
- **Weight:** ~4.5/5 (among the 10 heaviest Euros on BGG)
- **Mechanics:** action-card selection with role-activation; clear-rubble / buy-ships / open-stores / build-plans; government influence (Prime Minister / Admiral / Cardinal) unlocks thresholds; residual VP from wigs, officials, favors, courtiers.
- **Scoring:** endgame state scoring against the queen + building bonuses + favor tracks + ships + courtiers.
- **BGG rank:** #25-40 all-time (varies); design weight 4.55.

## Why Lisboa for game #11

1. **First Lacerda-on-Lacerda anchor.** Calibration: does the rubric recognize Lacerda's canonical signature when he reviews it himself? B1 is explicitly Lacerda's primary axis AND Lisboa is explicitly the canonical anchor-10 exemplar. The pairing is the strongest possible test of the designer-on-own pattern.
2. **B4 Info-Transparency-Cost second anchor opportunity.** Lisboa is famously information-dense: card hands are hidden, timing of officials triggers hidden, future plans hidden. Lacerda-primary axis #2 should earn decisively. B4 is adopted; this is a re-earning case.
3. **A7 Emergence-Replayability canonical.** 15+ plays reveal new strategic veins (wig-rush, courtier-stack, ship-rush, plan-heavy, official-chain). Another adopted re-earn.
4. **B3 Conversion Chain Depth.** Rubble → ships → goods → stores → favors → VP. Deep chain with 4-5 transformations. Adopted re-earn.
5. **D4 Late-Game Lock-in.** Final third is execution; mid-game builds commit paths. Adopted re-earn.
6. **A6 Teachability retire-explicit probable.** Lisboa teach is 45-60+ min; retire-explicit on adopted axis.
7. **D1 Family-to-Expert retire-explicit near-certain.** Expert-only; 9-year-olds don't play Lisboa.
8. **A4 Variance Calibration diagnostic-low.** No dice, only card draw; Vaccarino probable low-stake.
9. **D3 Count-Robustness diagnostic.** 2p is a different game (dummy player / nobility); 3p optimal; 4p adds 60+ min. Stegmaier's axis gets a stress-case.

## Artifact-as-story

The **card-hand + board-state + favor tracks** is the centerpiece. Each player plays cards to move officials, clear rubble squares, build shops on rebuilt blocks, commission ships, and earn favors. Every card move simultaneously advances the player's economy AND shifts the government influence tracks AND may trigger scoring thresholds. The machine has one interior.
