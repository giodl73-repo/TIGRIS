# Gloomhaven — Concept

## Premise

Gloomhaven is a 1–4 player legacy dungeon-crawl cooperative game where players control mercenaries battling through 95 scenarios. Card-hand management is the combat engine: each round players select 2 cards from their hand and perform the top action of one and bottom action of the other. Initiative, movement, and special abilities emerge from the card pair selection.

Stegmaier anchors on A6 Teachability — despite the game's notorious complexity (95 scenarios, 17 character classes, hundreds of enemies), the first session is comprehensible because combat resolves from card pairs with clear iconography.
