- **Dominion** — Donald X. Vaccarino, Rio Grande Games (2008). Spiel des Jahres 2009 winner.
- 2–4 players; 30 min; weight ~2.3/5.

## Artifact-as-story

Dominion's centerpiece is **the deck itself** — every player builds their own deck across the game. Unlike Parliament's shared Axis Pool or T&E's emergent map, Dominion's artifact is *per-player, built-during-play*. Players shuffle and re-shuffle; the deck's composition is known only to the owner.
