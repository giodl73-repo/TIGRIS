## Protocol amendment adoption (cycle 2 closing)

**I-parliament-03 — Anchor-axis-without-partner GATE check: ADOPTED.**

Three cluster instances (P, T&E, FACETS). Dominion provided a positive counter-example showing the rule would pass naturally when personas coordinate (cross-player partner drafted). Cluster strength + recent positive evidence = adopt now. Consumes cycle-2 rate limit budget.

**Amendment:** The GATE check (v2.3) adds a fourth condition:

> 4. **Anchor adjacency partner present.** The anchor axis has at least one semantically-adjacent axis drafted by a DIFFERENT persona. If the anchor persona drafts both the anchor axis and its adjacency partner, the GATE fails unless no other persona can draft the adjacency partner (explicit exemption).

Applied to Dominion retrospectively: PASSES (Chvátil on B5, Lacerda on A7 — different personas).
Applied to prior games retrospectively: would have required re-draft on Parliament (Feld drafted C6+A2 = anchor+partner, same player... wait Feld was anchor on C6 in FACETS. Parliament and T&E had Knizia as anchor on C2; C2's partner is B3 which was undrafted. Both Parliament and T&E would have failed the new GATE.) Historical application note: v2.3 GATE applies to games from this point forward only (forward-only versioning).
