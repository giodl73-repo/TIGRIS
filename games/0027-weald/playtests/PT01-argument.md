## Round 2 — First Displacement

**P2's key action**: Displace P1's Manor from Road-junction Hex 8. P2 pays base cost (1 Gold) + 1 (any resource; pays 1 Food). P2 places their own Workshop.

Wait — P2 places a Workshop (3 VP + 2 Road-junction = 5 VP) instead of a Manor? No — P2 wants to deny P1 the 7 VP hex, not necessarily maximize their own VP. P2 places a Mill (4 VP + 2 = 6 VP).

P1's Manor is returned to their hand. P1 loses the 7 VP position.
