### I-weald-03 — Displacement as A3 counter-pressure mechanism; mechanical > social > informational

- **Dimension:** A3 Interaction; counter-pressure mechanism taxonomy
- **Trigger pattern:** a3_counter_pressure_mechanism_new_type
- **Source:** games/0027-weald
- **Scope:** pattern-across-games
- **Status:** observational
- **Note:** A3 counter-pressure history now shows 3 mechanism types: (1) Vigil/Covenstat cycle 1: mandatory discussion + shared-information — social mechanisms; (2) WEALD cycle 3: displacement — mechanical mechanisms. Social mechanisms are harder to design (need thematic justification) and easier to fake (could become optional). Mechanical mechanisms are cleaner: the rules FORCE the interaction. For future A3 counter-pressure designs, mechanical mandatory interaction (like displacement, bumping, contesting) is stronger evidence than social mandatory interaction.
