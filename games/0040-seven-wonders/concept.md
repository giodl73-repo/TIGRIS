# 7 Wonders — Concept

## Premise

7 Wonders is a 2–7 player simultaneous card-drafting civilization game. Each of 3 Ages, players simultaneously choose one card from a hand of 7, pass the remaining hand to a neighbor, and resolve. No waiting: all 7 players act at once. The Wonder board gives each player a unique asymmetric power structure and scoring path.

K-K anchors on A5 Downtime/Pacing — simultaneous play eliminates downtime entirely; 7 players resolve a 30-minute game with essentially zero wait.

## Players and length

- **Players:** 2–7
- **Length:** 30–45 min
- **Weight:** 2.32

## Anchor

**K-K on A5 Downtime/Pacing** — simultaneous drafting is the canonical zero-downtime mechanism. Every player is always active; hands pass simultaneously. The wait between meaningful player activity is structurally zero.

**Collision candidate: A5↔A6** — does simultaneous play (A5) make teaching harder (A6)? Or does it make teaching easier because there's no dead time to confuse new players?

## Target

Simultaneous drafting / civilization gap closed. K-K own-anchor on A5 (Spiel des Jahres–adjacent design).
