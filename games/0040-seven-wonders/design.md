# 7 Wonders — Design (Rules Import)

## Core loop

3 Ages of 6 rounds each. Each round: choose 1 card from hand → all players reveal simultaneously → resolve. Pass remaining hand left (Ages 1, 3) or right (Age 2).

## Card types

- **Brown/Gray (Resource):** produce resources each turn.
- **Blue (Civic):** flat VP.
- **Yellow (Commerce):** trading and economic bonuses.
- **Green (Science):** scoring via symbol sets (symbols² + 7 per set-of-3).
- **Red (Military):** conflict tokens at Age end vs. neighbors.
- **Purple (Guild, Age 3 only):** conditional VP based on neighbors' boards.

## Wonder boards

7 Wonders each provide 2–3 stages of construction. Each stage costs resources (buried face-down from hand). Wonders provide unique abilities: extra turns, guild-copying, commerce bonuses, military immunity.

## Scoring

Sum of: civic VPs, military tokens (win = +1/+3/+5 per Age; loss = −1), science (symbols² + 7/set-of-3), commerce bonuses, guild cards, wonder stages, coin VPs (3 coins = 1 VP). Winner = highest total.

## Key mechanisms

- **Drafting:** hand passed each round = you must consider what to pass as much as what to keep.
- **Neighbor dependency:** military and guild scoring depends entirely on left/right neighbors.
- **Simultaneous resolution:** all choices revealed at once; no turn order advantage.

## Weight profile

Weight 2.32. Teach time ~15 minutes for new players; returning players 5 min refresher. Scales 2–7 (with Leaders, Cities expansions).
