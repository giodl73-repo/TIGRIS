# Tier-A Stakes — Ra (BGG 12)

## Anchor

**Knizia** anchors **C1 Tension Budget** (adopted). Claim: "Sun tokens ARE bidding power AND end-game VP simultaneously. Every bid spends the resource that scores you at the end. The tension is structural: you cannot bid without costing yourself. Perpetual scarcity by design — you're always holding fewer suns than you'd like while watching the auction lot grow."

Adjacency partner: **Vaccarino on A2** (different persona). C1↔A2 is the designed OP collision: tension of the auction moment (C1) vs. per-bid calculation density (A2).
