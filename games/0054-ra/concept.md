# Ra — Concept (Review Mode)

## Published game summary

Ra is a tile-drafting auction game for 2–5 players (45–90 min, weight 2.7). Players bid sun tokens to acquire tiles across three epochs. Each epoch advances via Ra tiles drawn from the bag; when the epoch track fills, the epoch ends. Players score based on tile sets collected. Held sun tokens score negatively at game end — bidding power costs you end-game VP. The tension is perpetual: high-value suns give bidding power but drain your end-game score.

**BGG rank:** in top 100 (BGG ID 12). Designer: Reiner Knizia. Publisher: Alea (1999). Knizia's most celebrated auction design.
