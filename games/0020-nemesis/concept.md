# Nemesis — Concept (review-import)

## Anchor declaration

**Anchor persona:** Chvátil
**Anchor axis:** B5 Architectural Novelty (adopted)
**Anchor claim:** "Nemesis's **Intruder mechanism** — fog-of-war enemy tokens with unrevealed threat levels + evolution progression + encounter-deck — is genuinely structurally novel for horror/survival games. Alien (1979) framing meets Kwapiński's card-driven enemy system. Chvátil 9 games since last anchor (TtA #8); strong B5 case."

**Secondary focus:** Horror coverage gap closure. TIGRIS corpus had 0 horror reviews pre-Nemesis; Vigil was the sole horror-original. Nemesis is the BGG-top-20 horror reference.

## Context

- 4th consecutive non-own anchor (Kwapiński not in 8-persona roster; follows Gupta/Matthews, Wallace, Hargrave).
- 1-5 players (including solo Automa). BGG weight 3.4. ~2-3 hours.
- **Semi-cooperative** — players can win together OR individually via personal objectives; traitor-adjacent but not a pure traitor game.
- Closes horror/sci-fi-horror coverage gap identified in BGG scan + gaps.md.

## Expected signals

**Earn-candidates:**
- **B5 Architectural Novelty (anchor; Chvátil)** — Intruder mechanism + fog-of-war card deck.
- **B2 Catastrophe Pressure (Feld)** — self-destruct timer + fire spread + contamination + Queen arrival. Canonical multi-catastrophe design.
- **A2 Decision Density (Feld)** — per-turn action menu (7+ actions) × Intruder-awareness × objective tracking.
- **B4 Information-Transparency-Cost (Lacerda)** — fog-of-war Intruder tokens + hidden personal objectives + noise-triggered encounters.
- **A4 Variance Calibration (Vaccarino)** — Intruder deck shuffle + event deck + encounter cards.
- **A3 Interaction (Chvátil)** — semi-coop with hidden-objective pressure. Note: A3 formally recovered to 4E/0R via v2.18; Nemesis provides ordinary canonical earning, not counter-pressure trigger.
- **A7 Emergence-Replayability (Lacerda)** — character × intruder × objective × room combinations vast. Note: Nemesis earning contributes to A7's ordinary ledger; counter-pressure trigger for v2.18 reserved for designed originals.

**Retire-candidates:**
- **C6 Point-Salad** — individual win/survive conditions; no aggregate score.
- **B3 Conversion Chain** — no conversion pipeline.
- **C3 Scarcity Bite** — survival mode is thematic, but scarcity is not the load-bearing axis.
- **D2 Spatial-Interaction** — there IS a spatial map, but intruder-fog-of-war is the spatial signature; partial.
- **C2 Min-Score Shape** — binary survive/escape.
