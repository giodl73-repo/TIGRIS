# Tier-A Stakes — NEST

## Anchor

**Feld** anchors **C6 Point-Salad Incommensurability** (adopted). Claim: "3 Decade Rubrics (Stability / Family Density / Legacy) fire sequentially and reward incommensurable tile-placement patterns. A board optimized for Age 30 Stability (dense, compact, central) is incompatible with the Age 70 Legacy rubric (isolated Legacy tiles, diagonal spread). No single placement strategy dominates all three rubrics. NEST's incommensurability is structurally designed, not incidental to the scoring dimensions."

Adjacency partner: **Chvátil on A3** (different persona). C6↔A3 is the designed collision: does NEST's parallel-board structure produce multiplayer-solitaire (A3 refuted by C6, Wingspan-pattern) or genuine inter-player interaction?
