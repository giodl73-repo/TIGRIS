## Argument Shape

UPSTAGE earns on the same E-band axes as RITE (A26, A27, A25) but from a completely
different design architecture. RITE earns via involuntary reaction and hidden stage
progression. UPSTAGE earns via voluntary commitment and public trigger cards.

The shared earn profile (A26+A27+A25 across both RITE and UPSTAGE) establishes a
PARLOR signature: social-physical games consistently earn this cluster. The PARLOR
pipeline's axis focus (E-band primary) is validated.

The DOUBLE mechanic earns A3 (direct cooperative interaction between two upstagers)
and drives the Star token's emotional weight (A25). It is the game's most important
design decision.
