# CODEX Founding Doctrines

Each School has 5 Founding Doctrines. At setup, each player draws 1 randomly from
their School's deck. Your Founding Doctrine is revealed before the Convocation draft —
it shapes which of the 25 Doctrine cards you chase.

**15 generated per School → scored → top 5 selected.**

---

## Scoring Rubric (max 25)

| Criterion | 1 | 3 | 5 |
|---|---|---|---|
| **L — Legibility** | Confusing edge cases | Clear after slow read | Memorised after one game |
| **S — Strategic direction** | Generic bonus | Shapes one decision | Defines a completely different engine |
| **I — Draft pull** | Draft same way regardless | Changes 1-2 draft picks | Changes all 4 draft picks |
| **B — Balance** | Dominant alone OR useless | Good but not game-warping | Only shines with right drafted Doctrines |
| **F — Flavor fit** | Could be any School | Feels like this School | Quintessentially this School |

## Setup Change

**Updated Convocation step 2:**
> Each player takes their School's Founding Doctrine deck (5 cards), shuffles it face-down,
> and draws 1 card. Place it face-up. This is your Founding Doctrine for this game.
> Return the other 4 cards to the box unseen.

**Draft impact:** Reveal Founding Doctrines before the Convocation draft begins.
Players can see all 4 Schools' starting positions and draft to complement or counter them.
