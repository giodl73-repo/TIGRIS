# CODEX Opening Slates

15 complete 4-player draft configurations. Each slate shows what every School ends up
with after Convocation, then scores the resulting game state.

## Card Reference (shorthand)

**Production:** IV=Iron Vein · TM=Tidal Mill · SC=Sanctum Chain · IB=Iron Bloom · LR=Living Root
**Conversion:** TX=Transmutation · GW=The Great Work · VG=Void Gateway · VA=Void Alchemy · SM=Stone Memory
**Spatial:** SW=Spider's Web · EL=Expansion Law · CG=Contested Ground · DT=Deep Territory · EN=Encirclement
**Temporal:** FH=The Final Hour · MO=Momentum · DL=Dawn Law · PI=Patient Iron · TL=Temporal Loop
**Conflict:** CD=Counter-Doctrine · LS=Legislative Strike · SE=Scorched Earth · RP=Raiding Party · TRB=Tribute Law

**Founding Doctrines (not in pool):**
- **Flame:** First to Burn (always first; first action free)
- **Tide:** Ebb and Flow (move 1 component/round)
- **Iron:** Tempered (Iron Tokens: +1 production per component built)
- **Earth:** Deep Roots (immune to Edicts; +2VP/component on matching terrain)

---

## Scoring Rubric (max 25)

| Criterion | 1 | 3 | 5 |
|---|---|---|---|
| **D — Diversity** | All 4 Schools feel the same | 2-3 distinct playstyles | 4 genuinely different engines |
| **CF — Conflict density** | Pure parallel play, no fights | Occasional collision | Fights over space inevitable |
| **EC — Engine coherence** | Doctrines don't interact for most Schools | Some Schools have synergies | All 4 Schools have clear internal logic |
| **B — Balance** | 1 School obviously dominant | Close but one advantage | No clear dominant strategy |
| **T — 90-min fit** | Will run 2+ hours | Might push 100 min | Comfortably 90 min |

## Recommended Playtest Sequence

1. **Slate 1** — Establish baseline. Learn the turn structure.
2. **Slate 5** — Stress-test the Veil pacing. Does 90 min hold?
3. **Slate 10** — Test Flame's tempo advantage. Is it fun or suffocating?
4. **Slate 3** — Test map collision. Do the Fault Lines feel good?
5. **Slate 7** — Iron balance diagnostic. If Iron wins by 8+, nerf Sanctum Chain or Iron Bloom.
6. **Slate 14** — Conflict diagnostic. If the pacifist game feels hollow, Conflict is load-bearing.
